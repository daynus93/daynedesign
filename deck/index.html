Case Study 01

Lessen Pro

Senior Product Designer | Lessen Inc. | iOS & Android | 0 → 1

Getting work done quickly, efficiently, and reliably. Lessen was running $4MM/month in revenue through emails, spreadsheets, and pen & paper. We built the platform to change that.

Lessen Pro hero
Problem & Context

What we were solving

Lessen was running $4MM/month in revenue through emails, texts, PDFs, and pen & paper. No transparency, variable costs, and no mobile platform for service professionals to manage their work.

Vendor experience story map
How Might We's
Process & Approach

How I approached it

  • User journey maps, shadows, and interviews with vendor owners, operators, and technicians
  • Personas, competitive analysis, then full IA mapping across work orders, scope, invoicing, and settings
  • Worked with 1 PM and 7+ engineers. Rapid iteration with Maze testing and SUS scoring

Case Study 02

Rivet

Design System Lead | Lessen Inc.

Guiding product decisions, one component at a time. Rivet was born from a need for consistency and reliability across the Lessen product ecosystem.

Design System Web & Mobile Cross-Team
Rivet hub
Problem & Approach

Context and how I tackled it

  • No shared component language. Teams rebuilding the same patterns differently across web and mobile
  • Positioned Rivet as an accelerator, not a library. Ship fast with quality and consistency baked in
  • Structured across Core (colors, type, styles), Web, Mobile, Copywriting, and Component Status
Color system
Key Work

From Figma to code

Every Rivet component starts as a Figma component, gets refined for look, feel, and interaction, then is documented before being translated to a reusable coded component.
