How I work

Three core principles.

The philosophy behind every project, every team, every problem.

01

Think ahead

With a decade of diverse industry experience, I bring expertise to solve problems with a focus on scalable, lasting solutions.

02

Fail early

I aim to reduce time commitments, enabling teams to quickly explore ideas without heavy investment in unproven designs.

03

Validate often

I strive to guide products toward testing whenever feasible, validating assumptions rather than leaving them unaddressed.
